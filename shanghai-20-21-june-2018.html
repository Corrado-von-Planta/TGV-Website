开发者见面会
True Global Ventures在纽约，旧金山，新加坡，北京，香港，上海，孟买，东京，斯德哥尔摩，伦敦，巴黎和柏林已经成功举办了26届金融科技活动。通过金融体系专家对金融科技宏观趋势的解读，成长阶段的金融科技初创公司对自己产品和服务的讲解，以及区块链从业者对未来趋势的判断，我们与全球金融机构一同探寻创新、变革、发展之道。
在今年的上海站，True Global Ventures会增加一个开发者见面会，提供给开发者一个坦率地交流探讨的机会。此次活动将会介绍3个涉及区块链，开源，GitHub和分布式账本技术的项目。参与者可以在整个会议期间询问项目背后的技术背景，与项目开发人员面对面讨论技术细节。此活动仅适用于开发者

TGV Developers Meet up

This will be a candid technical discussion where the speaker who is presenting a project can be questioned throughout the session about underlying tech behind the project. 19:10 – 19:40 Opensource projects
* Kai Ansaari, Project Lead, Bountysource (Melbourne)
 
Bountysource is a platform for anyone to post bounties on issues for open-source projects, and for developers to solve and earn money. Bountysource has a community of 46,000 open-source developers (growing by 10,000 every year) ready to solve issues and claim bounties on open-source projects. Customers include IBM, Facebook, Ripple and Google. Bounties can be paid in fiat, Bitcoin, Ripple, Mastercoin with plans to support Dash and Ether soon.

19:50 - 20:20 Innovation in InsurTech
* William Jin, CTO, Global Risk Exchange (Shanghai)
 
Global Risk Exchange (GRE) is a decentralized blockchain based risk exchange platform. Its purpose is to enable individuals, companies and organizations to freely hedge, transfer and trade on their risks.
 
GRE leverages blockchain technology and smart contracts to create a decentralized risk contract exchange. GRE makes risk pricing, transferring, and trading both easier and more accessible to users all around the globe. Through providing more competitive and transparent pricing, and a significantly faster claim process. As well as by stripping away the intermediaries present in traditional insurance and derivatives markets.
 
20:20 – 20:30 Networking and Refreshments

20:30 – 21:00 How Decentralization and Blockchain Are Shaping the New Internet
* Pavel Bains, CEO, Bluzelle (Singapore & Vancouver)
 
Bluzelle provides an on-demand, decentralized database network that gives applications better performance, greater reliability and massive scalability. To ensure developers get the highest throughout in performance, reliability and scalability, Bluzelle implements swarming technologies. A swarm is a large group of nodes (computers) that work together to store and manage data. Nodes in these swarms can go down and new nodes can come up with minimal impact on the network. Overall Bluzelle is a meta-swarm comprised of multiple swarms. Bluzelle has worked with AIA, Maybank, Temenos, BT, KPMG and more.
 
Bluzelle’s founders (Pavel Bains and Neeraj Murarka) are of Indian origin whose parents immigrated to Canada. Bluzelle now has offices in Singapore and Vancouver and successfully completed a $19.5M ICO in January.
 
Some key highlights:
Named a technology pioneer by the World Economic Forum
Named a top blockchain vendor by Forrester
Partnerships with JD.com, British Telecom and top blockchain companies Zilliqa, 0x, Bee Token and more.
