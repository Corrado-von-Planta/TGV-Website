48th True Global Ventures Conference COVID19 Winners conference - 9-10 June 2020
True Global Ventures has held 47 highly praised Disruption in Financial Services events in San Francisco, New York, San Francisco, Singapore, Beijing, Hong Kong, Shanghai, Taipei, Mumbai, Tokyo, Seoul, Zurich, Stockholm, London, Paris and Berlin, Warsaw,  Riyadh. We are stringing together Blockchain trends and what Financial Institutions in the future might look like while bringing the industry together.
 
We do not allow any of our events to become PR & Marketing event, as media and journalists are not allowed, and Chatham rules apply. We are aggressively challenging the ongoing hype around Blockchain. We dig into real user cases of Blockchain applications to solve real problems and define what should go into production or Blockchain technology.
 
These Rules Apply:
  • Chatham House Rules
  • No media, no press, no journalists
  • We do not invite intermediaries as well.​

Agenda (In Hong Kong/Taipei/Shanghai/Singapore Time zone)

20:00 – 20:20: Opening Fireside Chat with a Serial Entrepreneur
  • Wong Joo Seng, Founder and CEO of Spark Systems and Partner of TGV
  • Moderated by Beatrice Lion, True Global Ventures
 
Spark Systems are builders of next generation intelligent, high speed and cost efficient trading platforms. We believe the current foreign exchange buy-sides can be significantly better served. Spark Systems is backed by major Tier 1 Global investment banks including Goldman Sachs. The company’s objective is to enhance usability, optimise performance and user experience by providing a stable and ultra-low latency market place for regional emerging market currency markets. The company has recently finalised a US$10.5 million Series B round valuing the company at US$70.5 million post-money. The round was led by Citi. Incoming with them are HSBC and OSK Ventures.
 
20:20 – 20:50: Contactless payments in a country with a billion people - India
  • Prabhu Ram, Managing Director and Group CEO at PaySwiff
  • Moderated by Florent Mamelle, Partner at TGV
 
PaySwiff is an Omni channel Payment solutions Provider in India that seamlessly enables Banks & Merchants to offer & accept all payment options available. With >100K+ Merchants & Banks including Syndicate Bank, Canara Bank, Axis Bank, UCO Bank as Customers, Payswiff aims to service 1 Million Merchants in next 24 months. Apart from its strong presence in India, Payswiff Solutions are also deployed in 10+ other countries in Asia Pacific, CIS & MENA regions. Payswiff partnered with Visa, is also one of the pioneers  in introducing Tap2Phone a novel way to convert any NFC enabled Android Phone as a Card Acceptance Device.

20:50 – 21:20: The future of discovering tomorrow’s music with technology
  • Mike McCready, Co-founder and CEO of Music Xray
  • Moderated by David Rose, Partner at TGV, Founder & Executive Chairman of Gust
 
Music Xray is a platform that helps the music industry identify high potential songs and music. Such include jingles you hear in advertisements & video games, to  scores of movies, bumpers & theme songs in TV shows, and some of the hit songs performed by your favourite artists. The company uses a combination of music analysis software, machine learning & crowd sourcing over industry professionals to create the best music filter in the world.  With over 1200 industry accounts including brands like Sony, Universal, Warner, Netflix, and HBO and a user base of over 400k artists, the platform is one of the most widely used in the industry.
 
21:20 – 21:50: The Next Generation Brain Health platform with AI
  • Kelly Choo, Co-founder and Chief Marketing Officer of Neeuro
  • Moderated by Frank Desvignes, Partner at TGV
 Neeuro is a Digital Brain Health company that utilises AI, Neurotechnology and Gamification to help people assess, train and analyse their cognitive functions to optimize their mental health and brain fitness. They have solutions that deal with areas such as ADHD, Anxiety, Pain and Cognitive Decline, amongst others. Neeuro has been awarded the World Summit Awards under the United Nations framework and by China Daily in the Top Innovative Product category. Well-received in India &  Singapore with more than 6,000 users, with over 30 partners across 18 cities.
