​50th True Global Ventures Conference:

Highly Digitalised Countries Post COVID 19 creating New Sectors such as the Intersection between Fintech and Gaming

​8-9 September 2020
50th True Global Ventures Conference: “Highly Digitalised Countries Post COVID19” 8-9 September 2020
True Global Ventures has held 49 highly praised Disruption in Financial Services events in San Francisco, New York, San Francisco, Singapore, Beijing, Hong Kong, Shanghai, Taipei, Mumbai, Tokyo, Seoul, Zurich, Stockholm, London, Paris and Berlin, Warsaw,  Riyadh. We are stringing together Blockchain trends and what Financial Institutions in the future might look like while bringing the industry together.
 
We do not allow any of our events to become PR & Marketing event, as media and journalists are not allowed, and Chatham rules apply. We are aggressively challenging the ongoing hype around Blockchain. We dig into real user cases of Blockchain applications to solve real problems and define what should go into production or Blockchain technology.
 
These Rules Apply:
  • Chatham House Rules
  • No media, no press, no journalists
  • We do not invite intermediaries as well.​

Theme: Highly Digitalised countries post COVID 19 creating new sectors such as the intersection between Fintech and Gaming

  • Online moderator: Valerie Hawley, Executive Director Sorbonne Centre for Artificial Intelligence Abu Dhabi, Partner at True Global Ventures (Dubai)
 
17:00 – 17:50: Where to and Where Not to Invest in a Post COVID 19 Environment?

TGV’s 50th Global Conference: 8-9 September 2020
Date: 8 September 2020 (Day 1)
Time: 16:00 – 18:00 CEST | 10:00 - 12:00 EST | 07:00 - 09:00 PST | 22:00 - 00:00 HK/Singapore time

Agenda
 
16:00 – 16:20: Foreseeti: Opening Address & Fireside Chat: Cybersecurity as a Winner in Today’s Accelerated Digitalisation
  • Kristian Hansson, Head of Sales and Partnership at Foreseeti (Stockholm)
  • Moderated by: Emanuel Lipschütz, Partner at True Global Ventures (Stockholm)
  • Online moderator: Niklas Ponnert, Partner at True Global Ventures (Beijing)
Foreseeti is Europe’s leading provider of Automated Threat Modeling and Attack Simulation solutions, is a Swedish technology company within Cyber Security. Foreseeti’s solution empowers IT decision makers to assess the resilience and risk exposure of their IT architectures and take proactive and effective action.
 
16:20 – 16:50: Aptiv.io: How alternative data and AI can help Financial Services firms find opportunities in a tight market?
  • Guy Mounier, Co-founder and CEO of Aptiv.io (New York)
  • Moderated by Kristian Hansson, Partner at TGV (Stockholm)
  • Online moderator: David Rose, Partner at TGV (New York)
- Competition is greater in a recession and sales resources are at a premium.
- Successful financial firms understand the need for efficient informed selling.
- Real-world example of an Asset Management Firm selling efficiently with alternative data.
 
16:50 – 17:30: Data Analytics and AI Use Cases in Healthcare and Energy
  • Tomasz Roszko, CTO of Opentopic Technologia (Bialystok)
  • Ben Goertzel, Chairman of Singularity Studio (Seattle)
  • Xavier Latil, CEO of the Blockchain Group (Paris)
  • Moderated by: Carl Johan Grandinson, Partner at True Global Ventures (Stockholm)
  • Online moderator: Frank Desvignes, Partner at True Global Ventures (Paris)
 
Opentopic is a team of software engineers, data scientists, DevOps, QA specialists located in Białystok, Poland who have supported development of numerous AI and data analytics projects. The team has worked with brand names like Slack & IBM integrating ChatBot technology to design conversations between users. They have completed projects in various verticals including insights, content curation, recommendation, analysis, and more recently price prediction & financial sentiments in stocks and digital currencies.
 
17:30 – 18:00: Successful Nordic’s Entrepreneur in Smart Homes and Delivery Platforms
  • Carl Johan Grandinson, Founder & Board Member of Glue Home (Stockholm)
  • Moderated by: Oscar Stibeck, Partner at True Global Ventures (Stockholm)
  • Online moderator: Erik Rosen, Partner at True Global Ventures (Stockholm)
 
Glue is a platform and ecosystem that enables smart, controlled in-home access for people, products and services. Glue offers a plug-and-play solution for retail, logistics and home services providers to solve last-mile delivery, which is the biggest e-commerce pain point for consumers and retailers alike.
